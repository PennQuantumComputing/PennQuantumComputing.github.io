About the Event's Organizers

Ethan Abraham

Ethan is a Physics major at Penn's class of 2024 and a co-president of the Penn Quantum Computing Club. His research interests include nanoscale physics and quantum mechanics. Currently, he conducts research under Professor Abraham Nitzan, focusing on how molecular geometry affects transport properties of molecules. In his free time, he enjoys reading, playing ice hockey, and skiing.

Tunga Bayrak

Tunga is a Computer Science Major in the Engineering School. His past research focused in Quantum Machine Learning, and Quantum Algorithms. He has been teaching Quantum Computing for the past 3 years, including high school students all around the world, and at Penn Quantum Club to Penn students.

Jimena Olivares Rivera

Jimena is a freshman from Guadalajara, Mexico, studying Chemistry and Computer Science under the VIPER program at Penn. She does research on computational chemistry and catalyst design under professor Andrew Zahrt. In her free time, she loves playing tennis and playing the guitar.

Waseem Faheem

Waseem Faheem is an Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering Student from Cairo, Egypt. He's a science enthusiast and a teacher back in his home university, tutoring in physics, mathematics, and programming. At UPenn, he's studying Electrodynamics, Quantum Mechanics, and Quantum Engineering. His main area of research is Semiconductor Spin qubits and NV-Centers Spin in Diamonds. In his free time he watches anime or reads philosophy books.
